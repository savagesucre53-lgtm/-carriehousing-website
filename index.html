How it works

A clear path from inquiry to application

Every property and screening process may differ. You will receive written instructions for the specific rental you are interested in.

1

Send a rental inquiry

Provide your preferred location, budget, move-in date, bedrooms, bathrooms, pets, and property type.

2

Receive matching rentals

Available options that fit your preferences can be sent directly by email or text for you to review.

3

Receive the fee disclosure

For applications requiring it, the written disclosure will state the $80 application fee, its purpose, payment method, and applicable refund policy.

4

Access and complete the application

After the required fee is confirmed, application access and secure submission instructions are provided.

5

Review and next steps

You will receive an approval, denial, or request for additional information. Approval is not guaranteed.

Frequently asked questions

What renters usually ask

These answers are general. Property-specific terms will be provided in writing.

Why are there no listings on the website?

Availability changes quickly. After you submit your preferences, matching rentals can be sent directly to you by email or text.

When is the $80 application fee paid?

Only after you receive the exact property address, written terms, the fee disclosure, and official payment instructions. The fee does not guarantee approval.

What should I include in my inquiry?

Your preferred city or state, maximum rent, move-in date, bedrooms, bathrooms, property type, and pet information.

Should I send sensitive documents through this form?

No. Do not submit Social Security numbers, bank credentials, card details, or identity documents through the general inquiry form.
