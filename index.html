Frequently asked questions

What applicants should know

How do I get the application form?

Email the official address with your name, phone number, property of interest, and move-in date. After the property and payment are verified, the form is emailed to you.

Is the $80 application fee refundable?

The written fee disclosure sent before payment will state whether the fee is refundable and what it covers. Payment does not guarantee approval.

When is the security deposit due?

Only after approval and after you receive the lease terms and an itemized move-in statement in writing.

When is first month's rent due?

The due date and payment instructions are provided to approved applicants with the lease and move-in instructions.

What documents may be required?

Requirements may include proof of identity, income, employment, and rental history. Secure submission instructions will be provided.

How long does review take?

Timing depends on document completeness and screening responses. You will be contacted if more information is needed.
