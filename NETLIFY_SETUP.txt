CASSIE HEN REALTY GROUP — NETLIFY FORM SETUP

After uploading this ZIP to Netlify:

1. Submit one test inquiry and one test rental application from the live website.
2. In Netlify, open the project and choose Forms.
3. Confirm that these two forms appear:
   - rental-inquiry
   - rental-application
4. Open Forms > Form notifications (or Notifications) > Add notification > Email notification.
5. Send notifications to: carriehen@protonmail.com
6. Test again and confirm both the Netlify submission and the email alert arrive.

Security:
- Do not ask applicants for Social Security numbers, bank credentials, card numbers, passwords, identity-document images, or crypto private keys through these forms.
- Netlify email notifications are configured in the dashboard, not inside the website code.
